Motions are proposals to change what the committee is doing. They require a second, and often a vote. Standard motions include:

  • Motion to open debate ‚Äî Begin debate on the topic or agenda.
  • Motion to set the agenda ‚Äî Choose the order of topics when multiple topics are on the floor.
  • Motion for a moderated caucus ‚Äî Timed debate with a speaking time; chair calls on speakers.

  • Motion to move to voting procedure ‚Äî End debate and vote on the resolution.
